Neurovizr

Discover the power of enhanced brain functioning with NeuroVizr®️, the innovative app designed to foster neuroplasticity and cognitive flexibility. Using a combination of light and sound, NeuroVizr®️ goes beyond traditional brain entrainment by stimulating your brain with dynamic and layered signals. Accessible via your smartphone, the NeuroVizr®️ app offers sessions that adapt to your needs, ensuring optimal mental well-being. The NeuroVizr®️ device is controlled through the app via Bluetooth, allowing you to unlock its full potential. Whether you’re aiming for better sleep, improved focus, reduced stress, or enhanced creativity, NeuroVizr®️ is on your side, helping you achieve a healthier, more vibrant mind every day.

The Brain Gym collection offers Light/Sound Brain Exercises to enhance Coordination, Endurance, Flexibility, and Strength. It includes Brain Builder, ideal for boosting neuroplastic capacity, vitality, or cognitive function; Brain Stabilizer, using frequencies (Alpha, Beta, Delta, Gamma, Theta) to support focus, cognitive processing, relaxation, organization, and creativity; and Brain Organizer, enhancing brain architecture by focusing on major networks like Emotional Flow, Feeling Me, Field Attention, Focused Attention, and Shifting Task. Engaging in the Brain Gym enhances the impact of Mind States sessions, helping users find their personalized plan and rhythm. Exclusive to NeuroVizr®️, the Mind States sessions represent a significant advancement in Brain Engagement applications, incorporating light choreography and soundscapes for an immersive experience. Each session has a technical “signal vector” to achieve targeted outcomes, similar to how a comedy movie can affect mood. Designed as neurological compositions, they prepare, elicit, guide, challenge, reinforce, and reward targeted neuroplastic changes, affecting both state and trait. The sessions, available in “Standard” and “Lite” versions, ensure sustained attention for effective changes, with the Lite version providing a gentler approach for lower brain fitness levels. The Better Sleep collection in NeuroVizr®️ enhances sleep quality by targeting themes associated with better sleep, aligning with the Circadian Cycle to harmonize sleep patterns. Managing stress and engaging in evening relaxation are crucial for restful sleep. Better Sleep sessions are divided into optimal times during the day for use, helping users transition to restful sleep. Users have reported noticeable improvements in sleep shortly after starting these sessions, while others experience gradual improvements over time. Each individual’s unique circumstances and history impact their sleep patterns, but aligning the Circadian Cycle is essential for achieving better sleep and overall health. These sessions can be combined with other NeuroVizr®️ collections to enhance their effectiveness. The Brain Optimizer collection in NeuroVizr®️ facilitates controlled changes in critical brain behavior, focusing on “unsticking” a stuck brain. It includes Brain Processor and Brain Dehabituator sessions. Brain Processor sessions, based on AIP and EMDR models, use bilateral light stimulation and soundscapes to enhance brain responsiveness and induce neuroplastic changes, available in 11-minute versions. Brain Dehabituator sessions target fixed brain frequency patterns using three steps: Disconnect, Reconnect, and Reinforce, helping release stuck patterns and guide the brain back to normal, adaptive responses.
